TOP -> DOWN:
============
 inside a <TD>, use a <BR>, then <DIV 'POSITION:ABSOLUTE'>

 <DIV 'FLOAT:LEFT; DISPLAY:TABLE-CELL'> CAPTION <BR> <DIV 'POSITION:ABSOLUTE'>

 <CELL> <BR/> <INLINE,ABSOLUTE> </></>


LEFT -> RIGHT:
==============
 inside a <DIV FLOAT=LEFT>, use a <DIV STYLE="POSITION:ABSOLUTE"> after captions

 <DIV 'DISPLAY:INLINE; POSITION:ABSOLUTE'>

  <INLINE> <CELL> </></> <INLINE,ABSOLUTE>
